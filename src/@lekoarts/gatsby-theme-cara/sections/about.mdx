## About Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo was born in 1452 in the town of Vinci, the illegitimate son of
Piero, a notary, and Caterina Lippi, a peasant. He showed remarkable art
skills from an early age. Today he is best known for his paintings, but
his resume also included engineer, inventor, stage designer, musician,
anatomist, scientist and more.
